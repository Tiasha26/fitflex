Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time. The difference will be prorated for the remaining period.

What's the cancellation policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. After that, you can cancel with a 30-day notice.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes, we offer 15% off all plans for students with valid ID.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year with valid documentation.
